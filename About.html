About Bexx Secret Garden

Bexx Secret Garden is a very private, boutique full-service catering, event planning, and design venue. Bexx Secret Garden is hidden behind Bexx's private residence in the heart of NOHO Arts District near Universal Studios in Los Angeles, CA.

We specialize in Afternoon Garden Tea Luncheons, parties, and custom cakes. We aslo specialize in themed Birthday celebrations and Intimate weddings, as well as small office and corporate events. We also rent the location for proudction fulming and privide special set design in the garden.

The arched gateway entrance opens to a large covered main patio garden, then into a bar and lounge area that are approximately 1,500 sq. ft. in total. Each area can be customized to your event.

Main Patio

  • Host up to 50 guests with cocktail service.
  • Host up to 30 buffet table seating.
  • Host up to 25 seated table service.

Bar Area

  • 15 lounge seating plus 8 bar seating.
  • 2-6 seat tabletops with some lounge and bar seating.

Hours of operation

  • Shown by appointment Only.
  • Tuesday through Thursday 10 am to 9 pm.
  • Friday through Sunday 10 am to 10 pm.
